The Goats

    The GOAT of F1 is a highly contentious topic among F1 viewers, with many self described experts looking distastefully down upon others with a differing opinion to them. Regardless, here are the top 5 objectively best (i.e my opinion) drivers of all time, in no particular order:
  • Michael Schumacher
  • Jim Clark
  • Max Verstappen
  • Zhou Guanyu
  • Aryton Senna
